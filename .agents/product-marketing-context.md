# Product Marketing Context

*Last updated: 2026-04-06*

## Product Overview
**One-liner:** The Forturro Group is a full-service real estate brokerage specializing in residential and commercial sales across the Grand Strand / Myrtle Beach, SC market.
**What it does:** Represents buyers and sellers in residential and commercial real estate transactions, with particular expertise in investment properties, manufactured home communities, multi-family, and land deals. Partners Jeff Forman and Joe Scaturro bring investor-grade analysis to every transaction.
**Product category:** Real estate brokerage / real estate sales team
**Product type:** Service (brokerage)
**Business model:** Commission-based (buyer/seller agent commissions on closed transactions)

## Target Audience
**Target clients:** Homebuyers (first-time and move-up), home sellers, real estate investors, land buyers, commercial property owners, senior living facility owners
**Decision-makers:** Property owners, investors, families relocating to Grand Strand
**Primary use case:** Buying or selling real estate in the Myrtle Beach / Grand Strand area with investor-level market insight
**Jobs to be done:**
- "I need to sell my property for top dollar with minimal hassle"
- "I need a Realtor who understands the numbers, not just the curb appeal"
- "I need help finding investment properties with real cash flow"
**Use cases:**
- Residential home sales (listings and buyer representation)
- Commercial property sales (multi-family, retail, office)
- Investment property analysis and acquisition
- Land deals and development parcels
- Manufactured home community transactions

## Problems & Pain Points
**Core problem:** Most agents don't understand investor math — they can't speak to NOI, cap rates, or seller financing structures. Sellers and investors need an agent who operates like a business partner, not a door opener.
**Why alternatives fall short:**
- Generic agents focus on showing houses, not analyzing deals
- Big-box brokerages treat every transaction the same regardless of complexity
- Most agents can't produce investor-grade marketing materials (OMs, financial summaries)
**What it costs them:** Mispriced listings, missed deal structures, longer days on market, leaving money on the table
**Emotional tension:** Fear of hiring the wrong agent and watching a deal fall apart or a property sit

## Competitive Landscape
**Direct:** Other Grand Strand real estate teams — often lack investment property expertise and commercial crossover
**Secondary:** FSBO / discount brokerages — lack marketing reach, deal structuring, and negotiation skill
**Indirect:** Out-of-market investors trying to buy remotely without local representation

## Differentiation
**Key differentiators:**
- Investor-grade analysis on every deal (cap rates, NOI, ARV, cash-on-cash)
- Commercial and residential crossover expertise
- Vertically integrated with Freehold Homes (land + manufactured home development)
- Tech-forward operations (automated marketing, CRM-driven follow-up, AI-powered market analysis)
- Professional-grade marketing materials (navy/gold/teal brand, offering memoranda, social campaigns)
**How we do it differently:** We treat every listing like a business asset — with real numbers, real analysis, and real marketing
**Why that's better:** Properties sell faster and at better prices when marketed to the right audience with the right data
**Why customers choose us:** Direct communication, deep market knowledge, and the ability to structure deals that others can't
